Interior Painting — ~1,200 sq ft

Builder beige throughout the home dates the interior. Fresh paint in a modern neutral (SW ProMar 200, 2 coats + primer) transforms every room buyers walk through.

Areas included:
  • Foyer — upstairs and down (scaffolding for vaulted ceilings)
  • Owners suite bedroom
  • Owners suite bath — touch-up
  • Guest bathroom + ceiling
  • Kitchen & pantry
  • Under-stove area
  • Porch off owners suite
  • Laundry window sill
  • Door trim touch-ups throughout
  • Attic access
$3,575 ($575 materials + $3,000 labor)
2

Flooring — Rockwood Oak Laminate, 1,389 sq ft

All carpet is being replaced with Rockwood Oak laminate. This is the single biggest visual upgrade — buyers notice flooring immediately.

Scope:
  • Remove all existing carpet (1,389 sq ft)
  • Install Rockwood Oak laminate ($0.99/sq ft)
  • 6 mil poly underlayment (6 rolls, $168)
  • White quarter round trim (50 pcs @ $4)
  • Zama transitions (10 pcs @ $18)
  • Flooring removal ($500)
$5,423 ($1,923 materials + $3,500 labor)
3

Pool Deck & Concrete Paint

The pool deck is faded and stained. Painting around the pool area gives the backyard — your biggest selling feature — a clean, finished look.

Scope:
  • Pool area deck: (33×24) minus pool (25×13) = ~467 sq ft
  • Side strip outside lanai: 3×33 = ~99 sq ft
  • Sidewalk section
  • Light prep + 2 coats
  • Back porch: clean only (no painting)
$1,425 ($225 materials + $1,200 labor)
4

Seawall & Plaster Repair

Vertical cracks in the seawall plaster are visible and will raise flags during buyer inspections. Patching and replastering eliminates a major objection point at minimal cost.

Scope:
  • Patch vertical cracks in seawall plaster
  • Replaster affected sections
  • Seal and finish to match existing
$750 ($150 materials + $600 labor)
5

Lighting & Electrical

Mismatched and burnt-out bulbs make the home feel neglected. All 22 recessed lights get matching bulbs, and select fixtures are being replaced to modernize key areas.

Scope:
  • 22 recessed bulbs — standardize to matching color temp (sizes TBD on-site)
  • New fixtures: dining room, staircase, front door
  • 2 Juliet balcony fixtures
  • 2 deck fan/light combos
  • Bulb budget: ~$150

Fixture selections pending — will be updated with specific models and pricing.

$950 ($450 materials + $500 labor)

Landscaping & Plants

Curb appeal is the first impression. Trees trimmed back significantly (not removed due to root systems), plus fresh plantings to clean up the front and sides.

Scope:
  • Trim existing trees way back (no removal)
  • Fresh mulch and plantings
  • General cleanup of beds and edging
$700 ($450 materials + $250 labor)

Misc Repairs

Small items that individually cost little but collectively make the home feel cared for and move-in ready.
